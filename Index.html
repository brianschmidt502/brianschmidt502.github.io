About The Band

We are Rhythm Giants, a 4-piece rock cover-band based in Downers Grove, IL. We specialize in the best rock & roll hits from the 1960's to present day.

We believe that cover bands should give their audiences a fun, exciting and memorable night. We are real musicians that have had years of stage experience in a wide variety of genres.

Our sound is 100% natural. Because of this, we find that the audience connects on a deeper level with the authenticity of our music. We don't need to use click tracks or fake backing audio tracks that other bands depend so heavily on. We show up on time, set up, rock out with the audience, and do it all over again.
